What is Nisab?

Reference rates: July 03, 2026 · Verify with your bank before decisions.

The minimum amount of wealth a Muslim must hold before Zakat becomes due.

Nisab is the threshold for Zakat — the value of 87.48g of gold or 612.36g of silver. If your net zakatable wealth, held for a full lunar year, exceeds the nisab, Zakat of 2.5% is due. Many scholars suggest the lower silver nisab.

Example

If the silver nisab is 60,000 and your net wealth is 900,000, Zakat (2.5%) is 22,500.
