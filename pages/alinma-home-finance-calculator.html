How is the Alinma Bank monthly payment calculated?

How much deposit do I need?

A larger deposit lowers your rate and total cost. Most lenders also add valuation, legal and registration fees on top of the deposit.

Can I reduce the total cost?

Yes — a bigger deposit, shorter term, and extra/early repayments all cut total interest. Check for any early-repayment charges first.
