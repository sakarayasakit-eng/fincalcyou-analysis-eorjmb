Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Pag-IBIG housing loan interest rate?

As a 2026 reference, Pag-IBIG subsidized rates range from about 3% (socialized, lowest tier) up to ~10% for larger loans, fixed for a chosen period. Rates rise with the loan amount and fixing term.

How much can I borrow from Pag-IBIG?

Up to around ₱6 million for qualified members, financing up to 100% of the appraised value within ceilings, for terms up to 30 years.

Who is eligible for a Pag-IBIG loan?

Active members with at least 24 monthly contributions, within the age and income rules. Both salaried and self-employed members can apply.

Is Pag-IBIG cheaper than a bank loan?

Usually yes — subsidized member rates and high LTV make Pag-IBIG cheaper than most bank home loans, especially for first-time and lower-income buyers.
