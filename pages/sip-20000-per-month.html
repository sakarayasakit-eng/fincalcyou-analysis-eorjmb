Frequently Asked Questions

How much will ₹20,000 SIP per month become?

At 12%, ₹20,000/month for 20 years grows to about ₹19,982,958 — roughly 4.2× the ₹4,800,000 invested. Estimate based on historical averages.

Is 12% guaranteed?

No — it's the long-term Nifty 50 average; actual returns vary and can be negative in any year.

What is a step-up SIP?

Raising your SIP yearly with income — it grows the corpus much faster. Use the step-up option in the calculator.

SIP or lump sum?

SIP averages your entry price (rupee-cost averaging) and suits regular savers; lump sum suits a one-time amount.
