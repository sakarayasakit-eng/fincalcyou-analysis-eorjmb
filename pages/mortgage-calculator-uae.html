Frequently Asked Questions

What is the current UAE mortgage rate?

Roughly 5.5%–7% p.a. as of April 2026. AED is USD-pegged, so rates follow the US Fed.

How much deposit do I need?

Expats: 20% minimum (15% for nationals) for homes under AED 5M, plus ~7–8% transaction costs.

Conventional vs Islamic finance?

Conventional charges interest; Islamic (Murabaha/Ijara) uses a profit margin or lease structure. Similar monthly cost, different structure.

Can expats get a mortgage?

Yes — with a residence visa, qualifying salary (often AED 15,000+), and bank statements. Max age at maturity usually 65–70.
