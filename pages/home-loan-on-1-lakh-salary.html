Frequently Asked Questions

How much home loan can I get on a ₹1,00,000 salary?

At an EMI of 50% of income (₹50,000/month), 8.5% for 20 years, roughly ₹5,761,542. Banks cap total EMIs at ~40–50% of net income; existing loans reduce this.

What is the FOIR rule?

Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio — banks limit your total monthly EMIs to ~40–50% of net income.

Does my credit score matter?

Yes — CIBIL 750+ improves eligibility and the rate, raising the loan you can afford.

How to increase eligibility?

Add a co-applicant's income, choose a longer tenure, clear existing loans, or improve your credit score.
