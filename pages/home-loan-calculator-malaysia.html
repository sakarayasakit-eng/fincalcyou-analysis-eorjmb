Frequently Asked Questions

What is the current home loan rate in Malaysia?

About 4%–4.7% p.a. (April 2026), variable with BNM's OPR (≈3%). Islamic financing is similar.

Conventional vs Islamic financing?

Conventional charges interest; Islamic uses a profit rate (often with a ceiling) via a sale or partnership structure — no riba.

What is MRTA?

Insurance that clears your loan on death or total disability. Premium can be financed into the loan; MLTA is an alternative.

How much down payment?

Usually 10% (90% financing) for your first two homes, plus legal fees and stamp duty.
