Tips to get more PHP for your money

  • Compare the total PHP received for your exact amount, not just the fee.
  • Sending to a GCash or Maya e-wallet is often cheaper and instant.
  • First-transfer promos (Remitly, Xoom) can win once — check the ongoing rate too.
  • Watch the exchange-rate margin, not just the upfront fee.
  • Send larger, less frequent transfers to spread fixed fees over more pesos.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes. Several services (including Wise and Remitly via partners) deposit directly to GCash or Maya e-wallets, which is usually fast and low-cost compared with cash pickup.
