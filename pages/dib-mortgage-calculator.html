Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Dubai Islamic Bank mortgage rate?

Dubai Islamic Bank home finance rates are around 6.00% p.a. as a 2026 reference. The AED is USD-pegged, so rates track the US Fed. Verify the current offer with the bank.

How is Dubai Islamic Bank monthly payment calculated?

Using the reducing-balance formula on the financed amount. Enter the property price, down payment, rate and tenure in the calculator above.

How much deposit do expats need?

Expats typically need a 20% minimum down payment for properties under AED 5M, plus ~7–8% transaction costs (DLD 4%, agent 2%, registration).

Is this Islamic finance?

Yes — Dubai Islamic Bank offers Shariah-compliant home finance (Ijara/Murabaha) with a profit rate instead of interest.
