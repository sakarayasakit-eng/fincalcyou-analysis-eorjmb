Tips to get more PKR for your money

  • Compare the total PKR received for your exact amount, not just the fee.
  • UAE exchange houses (LuLu, Al Ansari) are often very competitive on this corridor.
  • A Roshan Digital Account can offer attractive rates and incentives for overseas Pakistanis.
  • Watch the exchange-rate margin, not just the upfront fee — that's where most cost hides.
  • Send larger, less frequent transfers to spread fixed fees over more rupees.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the live AED to PKR rate today?

Rates change continuously — use the converter above for the current mid-market AED/PKR rate. What your recipient gets depends on the provider's margin and fee on top of that rate.

What is the cheapest way to send money from UAE to Pakistan?

UAE exchange houses like LuLu and Al Ansari are usually very competitive on this corridor, along with Wise and Remitly. A Roshan Digital Account suits overseas Pakistanis. Compare the total PKR received.

What is a Roshan Digital Account?

A State Bank of Pakistan scheme letting overseas Pakistanis send and invest funds directly into PKR or foreign-currency accounts, often with attractive rates and incentives — no Pakistan visit needed.

Why is my rate worse than Google's?

Google shows the mid-market rate. Providers add a margin and sometimes a fee, so your effective rate is slightly lower. Compare the final PKR amount across two or three options.

How do I get the best AED–PKR rate?

Compare total received across an exchange house, Wise/Remitly and a Roshan Digital Account, watch the margin not just the fee, and send larger amounts less often to dilute fixed costs.
