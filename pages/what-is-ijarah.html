What is Ijarah?

Reference rates: July 03, 2026 · Verify with your bank before decisions.

An Islamic lease-to-own financing structure with no interest (riba).

In Ijarah, the bank buys an asset and leases it to you for a fixed monthly rental; ownership transfers at the end. It's used for Shariah-compliant car and home finance — the cost is a transparent rental, not interest.

Example

Meezan Car Ijarah finances a vehicle as a lease rather than an interest loan.
