Frequently Asked Questions

How does Islamic home finance work?

Murabaha: the bank buys and resells the home at a fixed margin. Ijara: the bank leases it to you with ownership transferring over time. No interest (riba) — a fixed, known installment.

What is the current profit rate?

About 6%–7% p.a. effective (April 2026). SAR is USD-pegged, so rates follow the US Fed and SAMA (≈4.5%).

What is REDF / Sakani?

A government program that subsidizes financing for eligible Saudi citizens. Check Sakani for your eligibility; this tool estimates the standard installment before subsidy.

How much down payment?

Up to 90% financing (10% down) for first homes under SAMA rules; more down means a lower installment.
