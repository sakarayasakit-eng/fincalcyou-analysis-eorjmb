About the scheme

Mera Pakistan Mera Ghar (MPMG) was a government-markup-subsidy housing scheme run with the State Bank of Pakistan, offering subsidized markup tiers on home financing for first homes. Scheme terms and availability have changed over time — confirm the current status and rates with SBP and participating banks (Meezan, HBL, Bank Alfalah and others) before applying.

How to estimate your installment

Whether under a subsidized scheme or standard financing, your monthly installment uses the reducing-balance formula. Enter your financed amount, the applicable markup rate, and tenure in the Pakistan home loan calculator to see your EMI and total markup.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mera Pakistan Mera Ghar?

A government housing-finance scheme (with the State Bank of Pakistan) that offered subsidized markup tiers on first-home financing through participating banks. Terms and availability have changed — confirm the current status with SBP and banks.

What markup rate applies?

Subsidized tiers were well below standard rates (single-digit in early years), versus ~15–17% for standard home financing. Check the latest scheme terms with your bank.

How is the monthly installment calculated?

Using the reducing-balance formula on the financed amount at the applicable markup. Enter your numbers in the Pakistan home loan calculator for an estimate.

Is Islamic (Shariah-compliant) financing available?

Yes — participating Islamic banks such as Meezan offer Shariah-compliant home financing structures alongside conventional options.
