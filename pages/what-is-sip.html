What is SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)?

Reference rates: July 03, 2026 · Verify with your bank before decisions.

Investing a fixed amount at regular intervals (usually monthly) into mutual funds or an index.

A SIP automates investing a set sum each month. It uses rupee/cost averaging — you buy more units when prices are low and fewer when high — and benefits from long-term compounding. Returns aren't guaranteed and vary with the market.

Example

₹10,000/month for 20 years at 12% grows to roughly ₹1 crore.
