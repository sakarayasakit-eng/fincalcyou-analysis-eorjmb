Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ICICI Bank FD interest rate?

ICICI Bank fixed-deposit rates are around 7.00% p.a. for 5-year tenures as a 2026 reference. Senior citizens typically get +0.50%. Rates vary by tenure.

How is ICICI Bank FD maturity calculated?

Maturity = P × (1 + r/4)^(4n) with quarterly compounding. Enter your amount, rate and tenure above for the exact maturity value.

Is FD interest taxable?

Yes, FD interest is taxable as per your income-tax slab in India, and TDS may apply above the threshold. Tax-saver 5-year FDs offer Section 80C benefit.

Is a bank FD safe?

Bank FDs carry no market risk and are insured by DICGC up to ₹5 lakh per depositor per bank.
