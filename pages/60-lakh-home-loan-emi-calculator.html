Frequently Asked Questions

What is the EMI for a ₹60 Lakh home loan?

At 8.5% for 20 years, the EMI on a ₹60 Lakh home loan is about ₹52,069, with ~₹6,496,655 total interest.

What salary do I need for a ₹60 Lakh home loan?

Banks keep the EMI within ~40–50% of net income (FOIR). Use the affordability tool to check eligibility for your salary.

Can I reduce the interest?

Yes — shorter tenure, larger down payment, and prepayments. Indian floating-rate loans have no prepayment penalty for individuals.

Fixed or floating rate?

Most Indian home loans are floating (RBI repo/EBLR-linked), so the EMI changes when the RBI moves rates.
