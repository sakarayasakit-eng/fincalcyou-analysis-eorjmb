Frequently Asked Questions

What is the EMI for a ₹1 Crore home loan?

At 8.5% p.a. for 20 years, the EMI on a ₹1 Crore home loan is about ₹86,782, with roughly ₹10,827,758 total interest. Shorter tenures raise the EMI but cut total interest sharply.

How much salary is needed for a ₹1 Crore home loan?

Banks typically lend so the EMI stays within 40–50% of your net monthly income (FOIR). Use the affordability tool to check your eligibility against your salary.

Can I reduce the interest?

Yes — a shorter tenure, a larger down payment, and early prepayments all cut total interest. Indian floating-rate home loans have no prepayment penalty for individuals.

Is the rate fixed or floating?

Most Indian home loans are floating, linked to the RBI repo (EBLR), so your EMI can change when the RBI moves rates.
