  • First-transfer promos (Remitly) can win once — check the ongoing rate afterwards.
  • Send larger, less frequent transfers to spread fixed fees over more rupees.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the live GBP to PKR rate today?

Rates move continuously — use the converter above for the current mid-market GBP/PKR rate. The amount your recipient gets depends on the provider's margin and fee on top of that rate.

What is the cheapest way to send money from the UK to Pakistan?

Wise (mid-market rate), Remitly and ACE Money Transfer are usually competitive for the UK→Pakistan corridor. Roshan Digital Account suits overseas Pakistanis. Compare the total PKR received for your amount.

What is a Roshan Digital Account?

An account scheme by the State Bank of Pakistan for overseas Pakistanis (NRPs) to send and invest money directly into PKR or foreign-currency accounts, often with attractive rates and incentives — no Pakistan visit required.

Why is the rate worse than Google's?

Google shows the mid-market rate. Transfer providers add a margin and sometimes a fee, so your effective rate is a little lower. Always compare the final PKR amount across providers.

How do I get the best GBP–PKR rate?

Compare total received across two or three providers, use corridor specialists and first-transfer promos, consider a Roshan Digital Account, and send larger amounts less frequently to dilute fixed fees.
