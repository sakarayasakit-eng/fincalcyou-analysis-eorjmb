📖 Finance Glossary

Plain-English definitions of common finance terms — EMI, SIP, fixed deposit, compound interest, nisab, Zakat, FOIR, repo rate, Ijarah, Murabaha and more. Free.

📖 What is Amortization?

The process of paying off a loan through scheduled payments split between interest and principal. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is Compound Interest?

Interest earned on both your original principal and the interest already accumulated. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is Down Payment?

The upfront portion of a purchase you pay yourself, with the rest financed by a loan. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is EMI (Equated Monthly Installment)?

A fixed monthly payment that repays a loan over its term, covering both interest and principal. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is Fixed Deposit (FD)?

A deposit locked with a bank for a fixed term at a guaranteed interest rate. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is FOIR (Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio)?

The share of your income that goes to loan EMIs — used by banks to decide how much you can borrow. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is Ijarah?

An Islamic lease-to-own financing structure with no interest (riba). Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is LTV (Loan to Value)?

The loan amount as a percentage of the property or asset value. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is Moratorium?

A pause on loan repayments — common in education loans during study. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is Murabaha?

An Islamic cost-plus financing structure where the bank resells an asset to you at a transparent profit margin. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is Mutual Fund?

A pooled investment that holds a diversified portfolio of stocks, bonds, or other assets. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is Nisab?

The minimum amount of wealth a Muslim must hold before Zakat becomes due. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is Repo Rate?

The interest rate at which a central bank lends to commercial banks — the anchor for loan rates. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)?

Investing a fixed amount at regular intervals (usually monthly) into mutual funds or an index. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.

📖 What is Zakat?

An annual 2.5% alms on qualifying wealth above the nisab — one of the Five Pillars of Islam. Plain-English definition with a worked example. Free finance glossary.
