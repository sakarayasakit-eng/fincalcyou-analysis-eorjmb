Frequently Asked Questions

What is the SBI home loan interest rate?

SBI home loan rates are around 8.50% p.a. as a 2026 reference, floating and linked to the RBI repo (EBLR). Your exact rate depends on your CIBIL score, loan amount and profile.

How is SBI home loan EMI calculated?

Using the reducing-balance formula P × r × (1+r)ⁿ ÷ ((1+r)ⁿ − 1). Enter your amount, rate and tenure in the calculator above for your exact EMI.

Can I prepay to save interest?

Yes. Partial prepayments early in the tenure remove years of interest. Floating-rate home loans in India have no prepayment penalty for individuals.

What tenure should I choose?

Up to 30 years is allowed; a longer tenure lowers the EMI but raises total interest. Keep it as short as your budget allows.
