Frequently Asked Questions

How much SIP to become a crorepati?

At 12% p.a., roughly ₹10,000/month for 20 years, ₹4,500/month for 25 years, or ₹2,100/month for 30 years reaches ₹1 crore. Starting earlier needs far less per month.

Is ₹1 crore enough?

It depends on your goals and inflation. ₹1 crore in 20 years is worth less in today's money — use the inflation-adjusted (real value) option to see purchasing power.

Which funds for long-term SIP?

Diversified equity / index funds suit long horizons. Past performance doesn't guarantee future returns; diversify and review periodically.

Can I increase SIP later?

Yes — a step-up SIP (raising the amount yearly) reaches ₹1 crore faster or lets you start with a smaller monthly amount.
