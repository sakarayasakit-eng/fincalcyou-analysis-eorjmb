Flat rate vs effective rate — read this first

  • Indonesian dealers usually advertise a flat (bunga flat) rate of 6–10%. The true effective (reducing-balance) rate is roughly 1.6–1.9× higher — about 9–14%.
  • This calculator uses the effective rate so the cicilan reflects real cost. Always ask for the effective rate before signing.
  • Bank Indonesia allows 0% minimum down payment in 2026, but a bigger DP lowers your cicilan and total interest.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is car loan cicilan calculated in Indonesia?

Reputable lenders use the reducing-balance (effective) method: P × r × (1+r)ⁿ ÷ ((1+r)ⁿ − 1). Dealers often quote a flat rate instead, which looks lower but costs more — convert to the effective rate to compare.

What is the difference between flat and effective car loan rates?

A flat rate charges interest on the full original amount for the whole term; an effective rate charges on the reducing balance. A 6–10% flat rate equals roughly a 9–14% effective rate — always compare on the effective basis.

What is the current KKB rate in Indonesia?

Flat rates are roughly 6–10% in 2026 (effective ≈ 9–14%). Providers include BCA Finance, Mandiri Tunas Finance and Adira Finance. New cars get lower rates than used.

How much down payment (uang muka) do I need?

Bank Indonesia permits 0% minimum DP under 2026 macroprudential rules, but most buyers pay 20–30% to reduce the monthly cicilan and total interest.

Can I pay off my car loan early?

Yes, though multifinance contracts often carry an early-settlement penalty. Read the penalty clause before signing — sometimes it offsets the interest you'd save.
