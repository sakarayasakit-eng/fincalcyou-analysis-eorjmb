Frequently Asked Questions

What is the UBL Drive car finance rate?

UBL Drive effective auto-finance rates are around 18.0% p.a. as a 2026 reference, reflecting the SBP policy rate (~11%) plus a bank markup. Verify the current rate with the bank.

How is UBL Drive installment calculated?

Using the reducing-balance method on the financed amount. Enter the car price, down payment, rate and tenure in the calculator above.

What are the SBP rules?

The SBP caps auto-finance tenure at 7 years and sets minimum down-payment and maximum-financing limits, which differ for locally assembled vs imported vehicles.

Is this Shariah-compliant?

This is conventional financing. For a Shariah-compliant option, see Meezan Car Ijarah.
