Frequently Asked Questions

Current car loan rate in India?

≈ 8.5–10% new, 10–12% used (2026). SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak are the major lenders.

Maximum tenure?

Up to 7 years for new cars; longer tenure = lower EMI but more total interest.

How much down payment?

Usually 10–20% (banks finance 80–90% of on-road price), plus insurance and registration.
