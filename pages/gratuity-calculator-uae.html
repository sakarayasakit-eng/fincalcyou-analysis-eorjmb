How UAE gratuity is calculated

  • First 5 years: 21 days of basic salary for each year.
  • After 5 years: 30 days of basic salary for each additional year.
  • Cap: total gratuity cannot exceed 2 years' basic salary.
  • Based on basic salary only (excludes housing/transport allowances).

Frequently Asked Questions

How is gratuity calculated in the UAE?

Under the 2022 UAE Labour Law, you get 21 days of basic salary for each of the first 5 years and 30 days for each year after, based on basic salary only, capped at 2 years' total pay. At least 1 year of service is required.

Is gratuity based on basic or total salary?

Basic salary only — housing, transport and other allowances are excluded from the end-of-service gratuity calculation.

Do I get gratuity if I resign?

Under the 2022 law, unlimited-style contracts pay full gratuity on the 21/30-day basis after 1 year, whether you resign or are terminated, subject to notice and the legal conditions. Confirm your specific case with MOHRE.

Is there a maximum gratuity?

Yes — total end-of-service gratuity is capped at the equivalent of 2 years' basic salary.
