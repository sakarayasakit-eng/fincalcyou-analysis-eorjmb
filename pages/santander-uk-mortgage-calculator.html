Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Santander UK mortgage rate?

Santander UK mortgage rates are around 4.55% p.a. as a 2026 reference. Reference 2026; BoE base 4%. Your exact rate depends on your profile and deposit.

How is the Santander UK monthly payment calculated?

Using the reducing-balance formula on the financed amount. Enter your amount, rate and term in the calculator above.

How much deposit do I need?

A larger deposit lowers your rate and total cost. Most lenders also add valuation, legal and registration fees on top of the deposit.

Can I reduce the total cost?

Yes — a bigger deposit, shorter term, and extra/early repayments all cut total interest. Check for any early-repayment charges first.
