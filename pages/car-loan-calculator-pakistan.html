The real cost of a PKR 4,000,000 car

Tip: A bigger down payment or shorter tenure noticeably reduces total markup. Compare conventional vs Ijarah on effective cost, not just the label.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the current car finance rate?

Roughly 16%–19% p.a. (April 2026): SBP policy ≈11% plus bank markup.

What are the SBP rules?

Max 7-year tenure plus down-payment and financing limits that differ for local vs imported cars.

Conventional vs Ijarah?

Conventional charges interest; Ijarah is a lease with a fixed monthly rental and no riba. Meezan and Al Baraka are common.

How much down payment?

Commonly 20–30% of the price; more down means a lower installment and less total markup.
